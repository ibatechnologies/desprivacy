Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/04/2025

D - CARE is developed by Diamond Auto Spare Parts to provide mobile access for battery warranty management. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

D - CARE may collect the following types of information:

  • Business-related Data: Information entered into the system (employee records, financial data, approvals, etc.).
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, and app version for performance monitoring.
  • Location Data: D - CARE collects location for logging user location during business activities. This data is securely stored and used only for business operations and reporting as authorized by the company.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) used to deliver push notifications.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and improve Warranty Management features and services.
  • Authenticate users and secure access.
  • Monitor system performance and fix issues.
  • Send important notifications related to Warranty operations.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.
  • Data may be shared with authorized system administrators for support or maintenance.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data:

  • All data transmission is encrypted (SSL/TLS).
  • Access is restricted to authorized users only.
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the system (subject to company policy).
  • Request correction of incorrect information.
  • Contact your administrator for any data-related queries.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be posted within the app or communicated through official channels.
